About Me

I have been working in the Los Angeles film industry for three years, as everything from a webseries producer to a storyboard artist. I live in Echo Park and I'm working on going back to school for programming and chemical engineering. My hope is to work in green technology, using the skills of each discipline for the benefit of the environment. You can reach me at ezRAez10_a_gmail.com or by offering a drawing idea on the home page!
